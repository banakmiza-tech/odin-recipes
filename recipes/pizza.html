Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of the most famous dishes in the world.

Absolutely everyone loves pizza, so will you!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Mix together the flour, water, yeast and salt.
  2. Knead the dough and let it rest for half an hour.
  3. Spread the dough on a flat surface and add the toppings.
  4. Put it in the oven on 220 C and bake approx. 4-6 minutes.
  5. Enjoy this great pizza.
